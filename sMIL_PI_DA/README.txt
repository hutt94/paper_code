How to Use?

1. download 'utils' and 'tools' from my homepage and extract them under the current directory.
2. install Mosek https://www.mosek.com/ (with free academic license) and modify the Mosek path in 'demo_sMIL_PI_DA.m' accordingly.
3. prepare your training and test data: 'training_visual_bags.mat', 'training_textual_bags.mat', 'test_labels.mat', 'test_features.mat'
4. run 'demo_sMIL_PI_DA.m'
